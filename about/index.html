Hi there! I'm Colby and I love helping people.

How? Well on a personal level I'm a motivator and a teacher who is always searching for better and more efficient ways to do things in life, and how to be happier. I help my friends to lose weight, destress, and get over tough situations in life.

For others, and you perhaps, I may be the perfect person to chat with about building a Meteor app, or anything Javascript and Front end related. I deal with performance issues and application design problems.

Overall, you could say I am a "professional problem solver." That sounds cheesy. Oh well :)

Currently...

At the moment I am a software designer for IBM, while doing a fair amount of consulting for Meteor and front end projects on the side. I tend to favor training and consulting over being hired to "just code things."

Let's get in touch!
